About Olivier

I’m a destination wedding photographer drawn to honest moments, timeless beauty, and the art of storytelling through light.

The story behind the lens

A life inspired by beauty and connection.

Photography found me early, but it was travel, people, and love stories that gave it meaning. For more than a decade, I’ve had the privilege of documenting weddings around the world — from the rolling hills of Provence to the lakes of Italy and the cliffs of the Amalfi Coast.

My work is about more than beautiful images. It’s about presence, trust, and creating an experience where you feel seen and celebrated. When you look back at your photos, I want you to feel the emotion, not just remember the moment.

Olivier Marceau
